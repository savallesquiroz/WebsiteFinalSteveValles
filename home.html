Public Transport

Madrid's public transport system is often considered one of the best in the world. The system includes not only the extensive Madrid Metro network, but also a comprehensive bus system, light rail, and commuter rail services. The public transport network in Madrid is well-integrated, with convenient connections between different modes of transport, making it easy for passengers to get around the city efficiently. The system is also affordable, with a variety of ticket options available to suit different needs and budgets
